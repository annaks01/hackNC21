Davis Library
Description
Located between Lenoir Dining Hall and the Student Union, Davis Library is a convenient and popular location for students to study. It stretches eight floors high and is known for being accommodating to various noise preferences: students usually go to lower floors if they prefer a chattier environment and higher floors if they prefer a quieter atmosphere. There are also study rooms available for reservation on floors 3-8.
